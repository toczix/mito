# Trouble shooting bloating

Sometimes it can be hard to ascertain why someone may be bloated - it could be dehydration, or poor diet related, or due to slow motility, or other factors entirely. Asking probing questions can help us uncover what may be underpinning it. Here I discuss the questions I ask my clients and how gathering this information can help us shape interventions.
