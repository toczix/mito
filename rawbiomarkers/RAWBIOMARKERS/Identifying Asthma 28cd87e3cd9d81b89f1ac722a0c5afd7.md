# Identifying Asthma

Asthma is a ***chronic inflammatory condition of the airways***, resulting in shortness of breath, coughing, and wheezing. 

Asthma may be triggered by exercise, weight gain, food allergens, pollution, inflammatory triggers, or infections.

**How does asthma show up in bloodwork?**

- Hemoglobin elevated (145+ for females, 155+ for males)
- Eosinophils elevated (0.3+)
- HCT elevated (48%+)
- RBC elevated (4.5+ for females, 4.9+ for males)
